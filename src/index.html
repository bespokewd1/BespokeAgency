Our Process

We Handle Everything, So You Don’t Have To

Once the contract is signed, we send over a short questionnaire about your business and what you offer. From there, we’ll handle the rest: we’ll write the content, design the layout, get your feedback, and then build your site. The whole process usually takes about 2-3 weeks.

  • Step 1

    Complete the Questionnaire

    We’ll email you a quick survey with fewer than 10 questions about your business, services, and what makes you unique. Fill it out, and we’ll take it from there.

  • Step 2

    Review and Approve the Design

    We’ll share the initial design draft over a video call, so you can see it firsthand. Any adjustments you need, we’ll make to ensure it’s exactly what you envision.

  • Step 3

    Development

    Once the design is approved, we’ll start building your site. Before it goes live, you’ll get a demo link to see the finished product. Then, we’re ready to launch!
